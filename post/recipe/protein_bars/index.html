Protein Bars

This recipe is based (heavily) upon one from none-other than the Doc himself - Greg Doucette. Mike and I have tweaked it slightly (mostly Mike - but he probably won’t read this so I’ll take the credit). This is definitely a good one if you’re planning on eating a lot of protein bars year-round!

It is on the pricey side the first time you order all of the ingredients, but if you’re planning on making them they pay for themselves. Much cheaper than splurging on Quest bars at the grocery store!
