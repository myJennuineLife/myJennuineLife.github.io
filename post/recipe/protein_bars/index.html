Protein Bars

Want a snack that’s healthy for you and tastes like you are eating a fudge brownie? If that’s a yes, then Combat Crunch is just the treat for you!

Mike and I love eating protein bars after a work-out, or as a mid-day snack to keep us satisfied until our next meal. While there are so many delicious protein bars out there (I am talking to you Quest Birthday Cake), they are not always budget friendly when you plan on eating them several times a week.

This recipe is based (heavily) upon one from the Doc himself - Greg Doucette. Mike and I have tweaked it slightly over time to what we prefer (mostly Mike - but he probably won’t read this so I’ll take the credit). We decided to name this recipe Combat Crunch, due to the Combat protein powder we use and the crunch we added. It is on the pricey side the first time you order all of the ingredients, but if you’re planning on making them year-round they pay for themselves. SO MUCH cheaper than splurging on Quest bars at the grocery store and you know exactly what is going into them!

The first time we made these protein bars I sent pictures to my friends and had so many requests for how we made them. Hopefully this recipe satisfies your protein bar craving and saves your wallet some cash over time!
